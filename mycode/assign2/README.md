For the extension, I implemented a rotary encoder and a buzzer. The rotary encoder turns 
clockwise to increase the time, and counterclockwise to decrease it. At the end of the timer,
the buzzer plays Twinkle Twinkle Little Star, although I configured it to play any song 
(within one octave) by simplay putting the notes together in a string. 

Through the extension, I learned how to use the internal pullup resisters, how to read 
information from a rotary encoder to determine direction, and how changing the timing 
of the signal for the buzzer results in a different frequency.
